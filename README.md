# AI-Driven-Business-Funding-Productivity-Analytics-Platform
This project is an end-to-end predictive analytics and intelligent decision-support platform designed to optimize small business funding, operational performance, and investment strategy using Machine Learning, Deep Learning, and Game Theory concepts. The platform aims to assist businesses, investors, and financial analysts in making data-driven decisions by forecasting business growth, evaluating funding attractiveness, and analyzing financial risks.The system integrates multiple Machine Learning models including Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, Support Vector Machines (SVM), XGBoost, and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to perform business performance prediction, productivity estimation, financial trend forecasting, and funding success analysis. Comparative model evaluation techniques were implemented to identify the most accurate and robust predictive approaches for different analytical tasks.To enhance strategic decision-making, Game Theory models were incorporated to simulate investor-business interactions and funding negotiations. These simulations help analyze strategic behavior, optimize funding allocation decisions, and evaluate competitive market scenarios affecting investment outcomes.The platform also includes a risk prediction and market volatility analysis module capable of forecasting financial uncertainties and identifying potential investment risks. Data preprocessing, feature engineering, feature selection, hyperparameter tuning, and K-Fold Cross Validation were extensively used to improve model accuracy, robustness, scalability, and generalization performance across multiple datasets.The project integrates data processing pipelines, predictive modeling, analytical reporting, and strategic simulation into a unified workflow for efficient business analytics and financial decision support. It demonstrates the practical application of Artificial Intelligence in solving real-world business and investment challenges through predictive analytics and strategic optimization.Additionally, the methodology, implementation, and experimental findings of the project contributed to a co-authored research publication, further validating the analytical approach and system design.
